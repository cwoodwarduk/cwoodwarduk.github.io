About Me

It all started in my teenage years being "that guy" who coded my friends' MySpace pages. I did all this from my whirring, spluttering first laptop, which I inherited from my father; spending hours googling various fixes that took me to the command line time and time again. And then at university I took things a step further by studying web design as out-of-department credits. The itch to code has been around ever since.

These days I am a full-stack developer and Agile-certified Scrum Master at Mosaic Digital Consultants, utilising my skills in PHP and JS within microservice architectures. I have a strong grounding in professional standards such as Git, TDD, OOP, use of frameworks and design patterns, and use of compilers in my workflow.

In my free time I enjoy cycling ridiculous distances for coffee and cake, cooking Asian food, eating too much, and making dad jokes. I also speak decent Korean, having spent 6 years in South Korea, and also make rice wine from time to time.
