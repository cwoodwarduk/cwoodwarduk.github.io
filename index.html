About Me

It all started in my teenage years being "that friend" who coded my friends' MySpace pages. I did all this from my whirring, spluttering first laptop, which I inherited from my father; spending hours googling various fixes that took me to the command line time and time again. And then at university I took things a step further by studying web design as out-of-department credits. The itch to code has been around ever since.

These days I am a systems developer and Agile-certified Scrum Master. My skills extend from scripting in PHP (Laravel, CodeIgniter) and JavaScript (vanilla, React) to cloud architecture (AWS with Terraform) and IoT platforms. I have a strong grounding in professional standards such as Git, TDD, OOP, PSR2 and use of frameworks and design patterns. Please check out my links above to find out more about me.

In my free time I enjoy cycling ridiculous distances for coffee and cake, cooking Asian food, and making dad jokes. I also speak decent Korean, having spent 6 years in South Korea, and also make rice wine from time to time.
