About Me

Starting out spening my teenage years coding my friends' MySpace profile pages (remember MySpace?), software languages have featured throughout my life so far.

From submitting university project work as websites on USB pens at a time when professors barely knew how projectors worked, to using data-driven insights to improve my performance through 8 years as a teacher; it's little wonder really that I chose to embark on a new career as a developer.

In my free time I enjoy cycling ridiculous distances for coffee and cake, cooking Asian food, eating too much, and reading. I also speak decent Korean, having spent 6 years in South Korea, and also make rice wine from time to time.
